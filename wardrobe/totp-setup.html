Two-Factor Authentication Setup

Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security to your account by requiring a verification code from your mobile device when you log in.

  1. Install an authenticator app on your mobile device (such as Google Authenticator, Authy, or Microsoft Authenticator)
  2. Scan the QR code below with your authenticator app
  3. Enter the 6-digit verification code from your app to enable two-factor authentication
